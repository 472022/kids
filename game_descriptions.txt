GAME DESCRIPTIONS & VISUAL GUIDES
=================================

1. MATH HERO (RPG Battle)
-------------------------
Objective: Defeat monsters by solving math problems.
Visual Style:
- Setting: A vibrant battle arena with a gradient blue sky, fluffy white clouds, mountains in the distance, and a grassy field in the foreground.
- Hero: A superhero character on the left wearing a blue suit, red cape, and yellow lightning emblem. He has an idle breathing animation.
- Enemy: Randomly spawns on the right. Can be a green Slime, a brown Bat, or a green Goblin with a club.
- UI: A semi-transparent "glass" panel at the top displays the math question (e.g., "4 + 3 = ?").
- Health Bars: Green (Hero) and Red (Enemy) bars float above the characters.

How to Play:
1. Look at the math problem at the top of the screen.
2. Three answer buttons appear at the bottom. Click the correct one.
3. Correct Answer: The Hero dashes across the screen and punches the enemy, dealing damage.
4. Wrong Answer: The Enemy attacks the Hero, reducing your health.
5. Win by reducing the Enemy's health to zero!

Winning Condition: 
- Defeat the enemy to see a "Victory! üèÜ" message and level up.


2. ALPHABET GAME (Bubble Sort)
------------------------------
Objective: Find the specific letter requested and put it in the toy basket.
Visual Style:
- Setting: A clean, bright background (white/cream).
- Objects: Colorful floating bubbles (Red, Teal, Yellow, Orange, Green) containing large white letters. They bob gently up and down.
- Target: A rectangular "Toy Basket" box at the bottom with a dashed orange border. It has a label like "Drag 'A' here!".

How to Play:
1. Read the instruction on the basket (e.g., "Drag 'A' here!").
2. Look for the floating bubble that contains that letter.
3. Click/Touch and drag the correct bubble into the basket.
4. If correct, the bubble pops with confetti!

Winning Condition:
- Successfully dragging the correct letter triggers a "Correct! üéâ" message and spawns new letters.


3. NUMBER JUMP (Infinite Runner)
--------------------------------
Objective: Guide the frog to jump only on the specific target numbered lily pad.
Visual Style:
- Setting: A side-scrolling view of a pond with a blue sky background.
- Character: A cute green frog with big eyes. It sits on lily pads and stretches its legs when jumping.
- Platforms: Green oval lily pads floating on the water. Each pad has a number (1-10) written on it.
- UI: A large instruction box at the top says "Jump on number: [X]".

How to Play:
1. The game tells you a target number (e.g., "Jump on number: 5").
2. Tap/Click on any visible lily pad that has that number.
3. The frog will leap in an arc to land on it.
4. The screen scrolls forward, revealing new pads.

Winning Condition:
- Keep jumping on the correct numbers to build a high score. Jumping on the wrong number warns you to "Try Again".


4. SHAPE BUILDER (Puzzle Match)
-------------------------------
Objective: Fit the shapes into their matching holes on the board.
Visual Style:
- Setting: Looks like a wooden table or floor.
- Board: A wooden puzzle board with indented "shadow" slots for shapes.
- Shapes: 3D-style colorful blocks (Square, Circle, Triangle, Star, Hexagon). Each shape has a cute smiley face üôÇ.
- Feedback: Shapes snap into place with a visual "pop" and confetti.

How to Play:
1. Look at the empty slots on the puzzle board.
2. Find the matching colored shape scattered around the table.
3. Drag the shape into its corresponding hole.
4. It will snap into place if it fits.

Winning Condition:
- Match all 3 shapes to clear the board and see "All Matched! üéâ". A new set of shapes will appear.


5. MEMORY FLIP (Card Match)
---------------------------
Objective: Find matching pairs of fruit cards.
Visual Style:
- Setting: Simple grid layout.
- Cards: Teal-colored cards with a white "?" mark when face down.
- Face Up: White cards showing fruit icons (Apple üçé, Banana üçå, Grapes üçá, Cherry üçí).

How to Play:
1. Click a card to flip it over and reveal the fruit.
2. Click a second card to try and find the matching fruit.
3. Match: Cards stay face up.
4. No Match: Cards flip back over after 1 second.

Winning Condition:
- Find all matching pairs to display "You won! üß†" and restart the game.
